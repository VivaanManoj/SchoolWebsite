  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    Having an Online Attendance Management System is a great way to monitor, record, and compile the attendance of students. The work becomes easy being automatic, and accessibility is also easier for compilation. This system monitors the classroom time and attendance of students in an organisation. This is a time and effort saver for the calculation of school hours. In a technology-driven era, automation is indispensable for managing every operation, so why skip this? Pens, registers, biometric devices, punch cards, RFID, or Identity Cards, in the case of schools are common. However, with the availability of everything online the automated attendance system is a really helpful tool even for the parents.
